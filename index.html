About GateFlow

GateFlow is a study companion designed specifically for GATE Computer Science aspirants. It helps students track syllabus progress, manage PYQs, monitor mock test scores, maintain study streaks, and analyze performance.

Information We Collect

GateFlow stores study-related information locally on your device, including:

  • Syllabus progress
  • Study streaks
  • Mock test scores
  • Subject test scores
  • User preferences

We do not require account creation and do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

Advertising

GateFlow may display advertisements through Google AdMob. These services may collect limited device and advertising information according to their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics (future releases)

Data Storage

Your preparation data is primarily stored locally on your device. We do not sell, rent, or share your data.

Children's Privacy

GateFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.
