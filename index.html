GateFlow Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Last Updated: June 2026

GateFlow is a study companion application designed for GATE Computer Science aspirants. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected while using the application.

1. Information We Collect

GateFlow is designed with privacy in mind. Most study-related information is stored locally on your device.

Information that may be stored includes:

  • Syllabus progress
  • Study streak data
  • Mock test scores
  • Subject test scores
  • Study preferences and settings

We do not require users to create an account and do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

The information stored within GateFlow is used solely to provide app functionality, including:

  • Tracking syllabus completion
  • Maintaining study streaks
  • Recording test performance
  • Generating preparation analytics

3. Advertising

GateFlow may display advertisements through third-party advertising providers such as Google AdMob.

These providers may collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers, advertising identifiers, and usage information, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

GateFlow may use third-party services including:

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Analytics (if enabled in future versions)

These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

5. Data Storage

Study data entered into GateFlow is primarily stored locally on the user's device.

Users are responsible for maintaining backups of their data where applicable.

6. Children's Privacy

GateFlow does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored by the application. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
